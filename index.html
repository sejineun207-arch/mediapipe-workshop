face-hand-vibe

Live face + hand tracking in the browser with p5.js and MediaPipe. Serve this repo locally, allow camera access, then open an example.

Starter

  • Boilerplate demo webcam feed with a nose dot and fingertip dots — edit sketch.js

Examples

  • 01 — particles follow nose particles drift toward your nose
  • 02 — pinch to draw pinch thumb and index to draw strokes
  • 03 — mouth controls tone mouth opens pitch; head tilt changes volume
  • 04 — two hands stretch image pinch distance scales and rotates an image
  • 05 — master controls live GUI inspector for signals and landmarks
  • 06 — data-flow graph TouchDesigner-style pipeline visualiser

Run locally: python3 -m http.server 8000 then open http://localhost:8000
